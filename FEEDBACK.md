# Feedback Requested

Thank you for trying Picture AAC Talker. This is a prototype for feedback, not a clinical recommendation.

Helpful feedback:

- What parts were confusing?
- Are the picture tiles large enough?
- Are the starter boards useful?
- What vocabulary is missing?
- Does it work well on iPad, iPhone, and computer?
- Would Group Chat be useful in therapy sessions?
- What room controls would a group session need?
- What would make setup easier for a caregiver, teacher, or SLP?
- What privacy or safety concerns do you notice?

Please avoid posting private medical details, phone numbers, addresses, or identifying information about a child.
